Chapter One · An invitation

Every good product we've built
started with a single email.

Not a form. Not a ticket. An email — from someone with an honest question, a half-formed idea, a hunch they couldn't shake. Some of those became partnerships. Some became products. Some became friendships that outlasted the work.

We don't have a call queue or an SDR. Just one inbox, one human, and a promise: whatever you write, someone (usually the founder) reads and replies personally, generally within the day. Below are a few of the ways people write to us. Pick the one that fits — or just say hello.
