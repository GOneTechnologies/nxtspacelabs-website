Legal · Privacy Policy

Privacy,
in plain English.

We treat your data the way we would want ours treated. This policy tells you exactly what we collect, why, how it is protected, and how you can control it.

Last updated · 3 July 2026

NextSpace Labs Private Limited ("NextSpace Labs", "we", "us", or "our") is a company incorporated under the laws of India with its registered office in Hyderabad, Telangana. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, retain and protect personal information when you use nxtspacelabs.com, our applications, and any related services (together, the "Services").

We designed our products and this policy around a simple idea: the user is the only stakeholder. If any part of this policy is unclear, write to us — connect@nxtspacelabs.com — and we will fix the language.

1. Who this applies to

This policy applies to anyone who visits our website, uses our products (including PairNow), applies for a role, or writes to us. If you interact with us in more than one capacity, the corresponding sections all apply.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you give us

  • Account information — name, phone number, email address, and profile details you provide when signing up for our products.
  • Communications — messages you send us through the contact form, email, or our support channels.
  • Verification information — identity documents, selfies, or voice samples where a product feature (like KYC for creators) requires them.
  • Payment information — collected and stored by our licensed payment processors, not by us directly.

2.2 Information we collect automatically

  • Device and log data — IP address, device model, operating system, browser, timestamps, and app or page interactions.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — see our Cookie Policy for the full detail.
  • Product telemetry — anonymous, aggregated usage signals that help us understand which parts of a product work and which do not.

2.3 Information from third parties

We may receive information from payment processors, identity-verification providers, SMS/OTP delivery partners, analytics services, and (with your consent) authentication providers like phone-number OTP services. We only receive what we need to run the feature you are using.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide, maintain and improve the Services.
  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To process payments and payouts.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including trust-and-safety enforcement.
  • To communicate with you about product updates, replies to your enquiries, and (only if you opt in) our writing.
  • To research and design better products — in aggregate, without identifying you.

4. Legal bases

We rely on one or more of the following legal bases: your consent, the performance of a contract, compliance with a legal obligation, protection of vital interests, and our legitimate interest in operating and improving the Services. Where the law of your country requires it, we ask for consent before processing.

5. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited situations:

  • Service providers who process data on our instruction (hosting, payments, identity, analytics, communications), under written contracts.
  • Legal & safety — where required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect people from imminent harm.
  • Corporate events — in the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition or restructuring, subject to protective safeguards.
  • With your consent — any other sharing is only after we ask, and always revocable.

6. Data retention

We keep personal information for as long as we need it to provide the Services or to meet legal, tax and accounting requirements. When we no longer need it, we delete or de-identify it. Specific retention windows are documented internally and available on request.

7. International transfers

Our infrastructure runs primarily in India and the European Union. If your data is transferred to a country other than yours, we rely on appropriate safeguards — including standard contractual clauses — to protect it.

8. Your rights

  • Access — ask us for a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to fix anything inaccurate.
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your account and information.
  • Portability — ask us for your data in a machine-readable form.
  • Objection & restriction — object to certain processing, or ask us to restrict it.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.
  • Complaint — you can complain to your local data-protection authority.

To exercise any of these, write to connect@nxtspacelabs.com. We reply personally.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security controls, including encryption in transit and at rest, hardened infrastructure, role-based access, audit logs, and periodic security reviews. No system is perfectly secure — if you notice a vulnerability, please write to us.

10. Children

Our products are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will announce them through the Service or by email. The "last updated" date at the top tells you when this version was last revised.
