7. Reporting copyright or trademark infringement

If you believe that content on our Website or inside a NextSpace Labs product infringes your copyright or trademark, please send a written notice that includes the following:

  • Your name, postal address, telephone number, and email address.
  • A description of the copyrighted work or trademark claimed to have been infringed.
  • The URL or precise location of the material claimed to be infringing.
  • A statement, made in good faith, that the use of the material is not authorised by the rights holder, its agent, or the law.
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are the rights holder or authorised to act on the rights holder’s behalf.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

Send the notice to:

  • Copyright / trademark notices: connect@nxtspacelabs.com with the subject line “IP Notice”.
  • Postal: NextSpace Labs Private Limited, Door No 1-60/8/A & B, 3rd Floor, KNR Square, Opp. The Platina, Gachibowli, Kondapur, Hyderabad — 500 032, Telangana, India.

We may forward valid notices to the party responsible for the material. Repeat infringers may lose access to our Services.
