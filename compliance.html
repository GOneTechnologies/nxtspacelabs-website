Chapter Two · Trust ledger

What we will not do.

Data

We will never sell personal data.

No exceptions, no anonymised loopholes, no bundled “partner offers”. If a business model would require it, we do not build that business.

Advertising

No third-party ad tech today.

We do not currently use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking pixels, or data brokers. Analytics is first-party and anonymised. If this ever changes, users will be told before it does.

Coercion

No dark patterns.

Consent is never bundled with function. Rejecting non-essential cookies takes the same clicks as accepting them.

See something off?

If any document here reads incorrectly, or any behaviour on our Website contradicts what we’ve committed to, write to us. Every report is reviewed and routed to the appropriate team.
