Chapter Two · How we work

Four rhythms, in order.

Every engagement follows the same four rhythms — but the tempo changes with the work. Some projects are weeks. Some are years. The order is always the same.

Step 01
Sit with the question.

Before we design or code, we listen. We find the real problem underneath the requested feature. Everything follows from what we learn here.

Step 02
Prototype in public.

We build small, working answers early — often within a week. Nothing convinces like a running product; nothing surfaces truth faster than a prototype.

Step 03
Ship, quietly.

We do not launch things. We ship them. Small, honest releases. Real users. Real telemetry. Real reactions. Then we iterate on what we learn.

Step 04
Hand it back.

We are not here to become the team you can't fire. We leave documentation, decisions, and a team that can carry the product further than we could.
