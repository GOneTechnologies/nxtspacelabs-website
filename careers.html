Chapter One · Who thrives here

Three honest tests.

No trick questions. If most of these sound like you, this will feel like the right room.

TEST · 01
You care about the last five percent.

The polish is not the icing. It is the load-bearing thing. You lose sleep over a badly-worded error state. You would rather ship less, better.

TEST · 02
You can hold the whole thing.

You are not looking to be handed a ticket. You want to own a product surface — its purpose, its behavior, its craft — and be trusted to make the calls.

TEST · 03
You believe quiet can win.

You do not need to broadcast the work. The work itself is the point. Applause is nice; a working product used by real people is nicer.

Chapter Three · How we hire

A calm, honest process.

From first email to signed offer, we aim to take no more than three weeks. No stunt interviews. No performative whiteboards. We look at your work, we build something small together, and we tell you the truth at every step.

  1. STEP 01
    You write. We read.

    An email, a note, or a link to what you have shipped. No cover letter required. Something small that shows how you think matters more than a resume.

  2. STEP 02
    A conversation.

    60 minutes with the founder. Not an interview — a conversation. What you are trying to build in your career. What we are trying to build here. Whether the two overlap.

  3. STEP 03
    A small working problem.

    A paid, honest piece of real work — usually a day or two, on a real product surface. No trick problems. If you are hired, this becomes your first ship.

  4. STEP 04
    Meet the team.

    Coffee or a call with two teammates. This is where you and we both decide if the day-to-day will feel right. Everyone gets a vote.

  5. STEP 05
    Offer. And a starting day.

    We move fast on the offer once we know. Compensation is competitive and honest. Equity is generous and vests fairly. We help you make the choice, not pressure you into it.
